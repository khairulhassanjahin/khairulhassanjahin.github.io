About Me

I am a development economist with a Master’s in Development Economics from South Asian University, New Delhi, and a BBA in Finance from Jagannath University, Dhaka. My academic background is enriched by hands-on research in climate resilience, urban sustainability, and development interventions. I have worked as a Research Assistant for GDRI and Monash University’s CDES, where I honed my expertise in randomized control trials and community-based research. My current focus lies at the intersection of ecological economics, degrowth, and sustainable development, driven by a passion for translating research into meaningful social impact
